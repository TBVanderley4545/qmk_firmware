Ty HHKB Layout:
===

This is my personal layout for my Tina-A keyboard. It utilizes a true HHKB layout.
---
UPDATE (4-28-2018):
===


---

Walkthrough:
---

This keymap has 1 base layer and 1 modifier layer:

BASE:
---

```
 /* BASE LAYER
 *
 * ,-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------.
 * | Esc |  1  |  2  |  3  |  4  |  5  |  6  |  7  |  8  |  9  |  0  |  -  |  =  |  \| | ~ ` |
 * |-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * | Tab    |  Q  |  W  |  E  |  R  |  T  |  Y  |  U  |  I  |  O  |  P  |  [  |  ]  |    \   |
 * |-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * | CTRL    |  A  |  S  |  D  |  F  |  G  |  H  |  J  |  K  |  L  |  ;  |  '  |    Enter    |
 * |-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * | Shift     |  Z  |  X  |  C  |  V  |  B  |  N  |  M  |  ,  |  .  |  /  |  RShift   |  FN |
 * |-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * |        Win  |      Alt  |            Space       |   Alt     |     Win                  |
 * `-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------'
 */
 ```
 
 LAYER 1 (FN):
 ---
 
 This is the only function layer currently set up. The goal of this was to mimic the HHKB function layer, but then add in extra functionality that doesn't exist.

 As I don't like using the lights very much, I just assigned the whole bottom of the board to control them because I don't typically use those keys in a function layer.
 
 ```
/* FN LAYER
 *
 *  ,---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 * | RESET   | F1   | F2   | F3   | F4    | F5    | F6    | F7   | F8    | F9   | F10   | F11   | F12   | INSERT  |  DEL |
 * |---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * | CAPS     | PREV | NEXT | PLAY  |    _  |    _  |    _  |    _  |    _   |  SCRLK |  PAUSE  |  UP   |    _    |  _   |
 * |---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * | CTRL       | VOLD  | VOLU  |  MUTE |   _   |   _    |   _  |  _  |   HOME    | PGUP | LEFT | RIGHT  |    _          |
 * |---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * | Shift         |RGB_MOD|RGB_HUI|RGB_HUD|RGB_SAI|RGB_SAD|RGB_VAI|RGB_VAD| END  |  PGDN  | DOWN |    _      |   _      |
 * |---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
 * |                    _    |      _     |             RGB_TOG           |     _      |     _                           |
 * `---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------'
 */
 ```
